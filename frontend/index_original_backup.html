Know your rights

Laws that protect you

IPC 498A
Protection from Cruelty by Husband or Relatives
Criminalises wilful cruelty by husband or his relatives. Non-bailable, cognizable offence. Up to 3 years imprisonment + fine. Police must act immediately.
Physical abuseMental crueltyDowry harassment
PWDVA
Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005
Civil remedies: Protection Orders, Residence Orders, Monetary Relief, Custody Orders. Emergency orders within 24 hours. You can stay in your home.
Protection orderStay at homeFinancial relief
POCSO
Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, 2012
Protects children under 18 from sexual assault and harassment. Child-friendly procedures. 7 years to life imprisonment. Identity fully protected.
Under 18Sexual offencesStrict penalties
IPC 354
Assault / Criminal Force Against Woman
Assault to outrage a woman's modesty: 1–5 years. Also covers stalking (354D) and voyeurism (354C). Non-bailable in serious cases.
AssaultStalkingHarassment
IPC 406
Criminal Breach of Trust — Streedhan Recovery
Protects a woman's Streedhan (jewellery, gifts). In-laws withholding it is a criminal offence. Complaint leads to court order for return of all property.
StreedhanJewelleryProperty rights
DV Act
Emergency Rights Under Domestic Violence Act
Emergency protection orders in 24 hours. Right to live in shared household. Right to maintenance and compensation. Right to free legal aid.
24-hr orderShared homeMaintenance
